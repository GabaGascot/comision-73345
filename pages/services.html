Services

We offer in-home care that keeps your pets happy in their own environment. Whether you have a curious cat, a playful pup, or a shy senior, we've got you covered.

Daily Visits Care (30 min)

  • Feeding and fresh water replenishment
  • Quick outdoor break or litter box maintenance
  • Engaging playtime and cuddles
  • Medication administration (if required)
  • Daily photo and update for your peace of mind

Overnight Stay

For pets that require extra attention or have special needs, our Overnight Care provides a comforting presence throughout the night.

  • Evening and morning feeding routines
  • Fresh water replenishment
  • Evening walk or litter box maintenance
  • Home care services: mail collection, plant watering, and light adjustments
  • Daily photo and update to keep you informed

Day Care

When you're away for the day, Pet Nanny's Day Care Services offer your pets a day filled with fun and companionship.

  • Feeding and water replenishment
  • Interactive play sessions and exercise
  • Medication administration (if necessary)
  • Regular updates with photos
